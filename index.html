Few things you should know:

There is actually no best time for weather in Mauritius, as it us usually warm all the year round. Average temperatures rarely drop below 24°C.

  • From January to April - This is the hottest and most humid time in Mauritius with temperatures getting into the low 30's. It does rain at this time of the year but rainfall tends to be in heavy and short lived showers.
  • From May to August - These are the ‘cooler' months in Mauritius, although the day time temperatures remain warm at around 25 degrees Celsius on average. The weather is ideal for children and those who don't like it too hot.
  • From September to December - Best time to visit Mauritius, the temperatures are warmer, traditionally between 27 and 30 degrees celsius, and humidity is not at its peak.
